Support, privacy, security, and legal questions.

Last updated: March 20, 2026. This page explains how to prepare a clear support, privacy, security, or legal request for the BitSend team.

Open Legal Pages Open Privacy
General support

General support and product questions

For general product questions or support requests, include the app version, platform, chain or network used, steps to reproduce, error messages, and any screenshots or logs that do not expose secrets.

Privacy requests

Access, correction, deletion, or objection

For privacy and data-rights requests, include the request type, relevant wallet address or app context, and your jurisdiction for rights handling.

Be specific about what you want reviewed, corrected, deleted, or explained.

Security reporting

Responsible disclosure

For security reports, include the affected component, version, reproduction steps or proof of concept, severity, potential impact, and mitigation suggestions if available.

Do not post seed phrases, private keys, or sensitive credentials in public reports.

Policy and legal

Terms and privacy questions

For legal or policy questions, read the Terms and Privacy pages first, then contact the BitSend team with enough context to explain the question clearly.

Response window

Replies depend on maintainer availability.

Response times are not fixed. For critical security issues, include the word Critical in the issue title so it can be prioritized.

Best route

Start with a clear summary.

A good request includes the problem, the product context, and only the details needed to investigate it safely.
