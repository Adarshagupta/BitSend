About BitSend

Crypto payments for real life.

BitSend is a phone-first crypto payment app built for in-person sends. It lets two people start the payment while they are together, then add the final chain result and receipt later when a phone gets back online.

Enter App Read Legal
What it does

BitSend is built around one simple payment flow.

The app focuses on a real in-person moment: two people are near each other, one wants to pay, and the connection is weak or inconsistent. BitSend keeps that first step short instead of forcing every chain action to happen at the same time.

  • Find the other phone nearby.
  • Move the payment while both people are together.
  • Save the final chain result and receipt later.
Why it is different

Made for the actual hand-to-hand payment moment.

Most crypto flows assume the network is ready the whole time. BitSend is designed for the more common real-world case where people want to finish the important part first and let the final online step happen afterward.

  • Phone-to-phone payment flow instead of desktop-style forms.
  • Local-first behavior with nearby transfer channels.
  • One clean record that keeps the payment and receipt together.
Under the hood

Mobile app, multichain rails, and receipt support.

BitSend is built with Flutter and uses backend services where needed for online settlement, receipt handling, and multichain support. The product is designed around a clean mobile-first interface instead of a generic wallet dashboard.

  • Flutter app with send, receive, activity, and settings flows.
  • Cloudflare Worker backend for connected operations.
  • Receipt publishing support for a clear after-send record.
